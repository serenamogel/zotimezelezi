Ananya Khare is an Indian film and television actress who is popularly known for the role played in the super hit movie “Devdas”. Whereas, she became famous for the role of in the film named “Chandni Bar” and fortunately, she was awarded the National Film Award for the Best Supporting Actress.

She was born 16th March 1968, Ratlam, Madhya Pradesh, India where she grew up along with siblings and parents. Ananya Khare studied from Jawaharlal Nehru University, Delhi, India from where she did post-graduation and earned a degree.

As she was so enthusiastic about acting in her childhood days so she participated in various school events to hone her gifted skills.

Career

Ananya Khare started her career on television and appeared in serials, such as; “Nirmala” (1987) and she worked on television for two decades before came into the spotlight on a big screen. She is undoubtedly a versatile and great actress who has won a plethora of awards and honors for her roles, stage performances, and television appearances as well.

She has worked in various movies and made a special place in millions of her fans’ hearts. Ananya Khare worked in “Zalim” (1994), “Shool” (1999), “Devdas”,(2002), “Chandi Bar”, (2001), “The Film” (2005), “Foreign” (2010), and “Sandeep Aur Pinky Faraar” (2019).

Also, she is mainly known for some negative roles played in films and television serials as well. She worked in various television serials, such as; “Who Apna Sa”, “Punar Vivah”, “Meri maa”,” Rang Rasiya” and others are worth sharing to watch.

Ananya initially worked as an English teacher at a reputed school before moved to Mumbai. While, in 2005, Ananya Khare took a break and moved to the USA where she met her husband, David.

Achievements

Among other awards, she won the National Film Award for the Best Supporting Actress in the film “Chandni Bar”. While she earned a nomination for Best Supporting Actress in “Devdas”.
