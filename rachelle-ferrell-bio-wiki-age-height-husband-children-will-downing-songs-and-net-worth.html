Rachelle Ferrell Biography

Rachelle Ferrell is a renowned American Vocalist, Musician, and Record Producer who was born and brought up in Berwyn, Pennsylvania. She is widely known for her success in the mainstream R&B, pop, gospel, and classical music scenes as well as her talents as a contemporary jazz singer.

Rachelle is also known for making outstanding appearances on jazz songs such as With Open Arms. Her most notable singles include ‘Nothing Has Ever Felt Like This’ (featuring Will Downing), ‘With Open Arms’, ‘I Can Explain’, ‘Peace On Earth’, ‘Run To Me’, ‘Gaia’ and ‘Inchworm’.
