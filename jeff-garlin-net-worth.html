Jeff Garlin Net Worth: Career &amp; Income

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

Jeff Garlin, an American actor, and stand-up comedian has a Net Worth of roughly $15 million as of 2023.

Jeff Garlin was born on June 5th of 1962 in Chicago, Illinois. He was raised in a Jewish household in Morton Grove.

His father Gene Garlin ran a plumbing supply business and his mother Carole Garlin used to work in community theatre while he was growing up.

At a young age, it was found that Jeff had Wolff-Parkinson- White syndrome, which is a heart problem that causes abnormally fast heartbeat.

Although the risk of complication is very low, Jeff had to stop playing sports as a child because of the syndrome.

When he was at the age of eight, Garlin was inspired to become a stand-up comedian after watching Jimmy Durante perform in Chicago.

When he was in sixth grade, his father sold his supply business and the family relocated to South Florida. Then he graduated from Nova High School, Davie, Florida in 1980.

After that, he attended Broward Community College, followed by a stint at the University of Miami studying film. However, he dropped out.

He began performing stand-up comedy there. In 1984, Garlin moved back to his hometown Chicago to pursue a stand-up comedy career.

With his hardwood and dedication, by the age of 22, he was living in Chicago and performing with the famous comedy troupe ‘The Second City’. During this period, he also worked with Stephen Colbert in the box office.
