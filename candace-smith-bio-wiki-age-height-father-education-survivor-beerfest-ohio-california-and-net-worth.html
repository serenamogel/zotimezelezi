Candace Smith Biography and Wiki

Born in Dayton, Ohio, Candace Smith (Candace Elizabeth Smith) is an American Lawyer, actress, producer, life coach, and love coach hailing from her hometown in Ohio. She is a graduate of the University of Dayton with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and a minor in Communications. She was also a contestant from Survivor: Tocantins.

Candace Smith Family and Education

Candace was born and raised in Dayton, Ohio, Her father was Ohio State Representative C.J. McLin. He passed from prostate cancer when Candace was only eleven years old. She was raised by a single mother.

Coming to her educational background, Smith is a graduate of Chaminade-Julienne High School. She has a B.A. degree in Psychology from the University of Dayton and a J.D. degree from Northwestern University in Chicago, Illinois.
