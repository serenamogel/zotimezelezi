G-Max Centiferno (move) - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

G-Max Centiferno (Japanese: キョダイヒャッカ Kyodai Hundred Flames) is a damage-dealing Fire-type G-Max Move introduced in Generation VIII. It is the exclusive G-Max Move of Gigantamax Centiskorch.

Effect

G-Max Centiferno inflicts damage and binds the target for four to five turns. This binding effect is similar to Fire Spin, except that it doesn't end prematurely if the user switches out or faints. Its power and whether it is a special or physical move depends on the move it is based on.
