FaZe Jarvis Bio, Wiki, Height, Weight, Age, Net Worth, Facts

FaZe Jarvis is an English gamer who became a member of the Call of Duty gaming group FaZe Clan in 2019. He is the brother of fellow FaZe Clan member FaZe Kay. Jarvis is known for making gaming videos, challenges, and real-life content featuring his older brother Frazier and the other members of FaZe Clan. He joined FaZe Clan in April 2019.

Jarvis uploaded his first video on YouTube on June 29, 2014 playing Call of Duty: Black Ops II. However, he didn’t show his appearance on camera until July 1, 2016 when he uploaded a video titled ‘WELCOME TO MY CHANNEL’.

Jarvis started making YouTube videos in 2018 but he was not very consistent. In mid-2018, he started to take YouTube seriously and started uploading Fortnite videos. In late 2019, he started making vlogs.

FaZe Jarvis Age

How old is FaZe Jarvis? He was born on November 11, 2001 in England. He is 20 years old, as of 2022. He holds British nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity.

FaZe Jarvis Height & Weight

How tall is FaZe Jarvis? He stands at a height of 5 feet 11 inches or 1.83 m. His body weight consists of 84 Kg or 185 lbs. He has dark brown hair color and his eye color is brown. His body size is 40-34-16 (chest-waist-bicep).
