How tall is Justin Timberlake compared to Britney Spears and Jessica Biel?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 21, 2024

After Britney Spears released her memoir, her relationship with her ex-boyfriend, Justin Timberlake, became a topic of conversation once again. Timberlake wasn’t painted in the greatest of lights, leading us to believe that maybe height isn’t as much of a synonym for a good boyfriend after all.

Recommended Videos

With that said, here’s where one of the most famous trios of all time stands in the height scale.

How tall is Justin Timberlake?

To no one’s surprise, some women just love a towering height in men. Marveling at their partner’s imposing stance is one of the many historically contentious claims made online by netizens who affirm men allegedly focus on weight, while women place emphasis on height.

If true, then that must have been the case for Biel and Spears when they fell head over heels for Timberlake, who stands at 6’1” (185cm). But there’s a catch.

Interestingly enough, upon meeting Timberlake, many fans have claimed that the singer is allegedly shorter than official records show. While some sources state that Timberlake is 6’1”, online discussions often dispute this, suggesting he’s around 5’10” (180cm). It wouldn’t be surprising, considering the number of men in Hollywood who tend to exaggerate their height, but we can’t confirm or deny it.

If true, while not incredibly tall, Timberlake’s height is still above the average for American males.

Similarly, both Jessica Biel and Britney Spears fall within the usual average for women, with Biel standing at 5′7″ (170cm) and Spears at 5’4” (163cm). Although the singer is slightly shorter, she’s often seen wearing heels that create the illusion of greater height.

All of these are merely fun facts because, in reality, the chances that we’ll ever see Timberlake and Spears sharing the same room are very slim.

Especially now, as the “Mirrors” singer is being asked to take his sexy back after the relentless and merciless internet found out all about his questionable acts towards Spears. Not so sexy, after all.
