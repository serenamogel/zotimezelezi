Mattie Arcand - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, September 14, 2024

First gained recognition for appearing on the YouTube channel Klem Family. Together, the family plays creative games together in competition on their Klem Family channel. She is also known for posting mini vlogs and "get ready with me" videos on her TikTok account. 

Before Fame

In 2020, she graduated from Franklin Community High School. She is studying nursing at Indiana University-Purdue University Indianapolis.    

Trivia

Her TikTok account mattiearcand6 has garnered 70,000 followers. One of her most popular YouTube videos features her and her siblings eating spicy food. It has garnered over 300,000 views. 

Family Life

She is engaged to TikTok star Ethan Klem. 

Associated With

In February 2023, she appeared in a YouTube video called "Marvel Trivia Challenge!" with Lauren Klem.
