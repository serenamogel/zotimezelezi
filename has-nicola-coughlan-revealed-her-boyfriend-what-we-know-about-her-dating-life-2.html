Nicola Coughlan Age Disclosed

Nicola Coughlan is 35 years old as of 2022. Coughlan was born in Galway on January 9, 1987.

She decided to be an actor at the age of five after seeing her elder sister participate in a school play.

Coughlan went to primary school at Scoil Mhuire and senior education at Calasanctius College. She graduated from the National University of Ireland, Galway, with an English and Classical Civilization degree.

Nicola went on to study acting at the Oxford School of Drama and the Birmingham School of Acting in England.

Details On Nicola Coughlan Career

Nicola Coughlan had an appearance in the action thriller film My Brother’s War when she was just ten years old.

She was recruited in the Netflix series Bridgerton in 2019, and the show will release in December 2020.

Coughlan and her Derry Girls co-stars performed a sketch with Saoirse Ronan for the RTÉ charity event RTÉ Does Comic Relief on June 26, 2020.
