Laura Leigh Net Worth: $250 Thousand

Laura Leigh is an American actress and reality television personality who has a net worth of $250 thousand. She is a graduate of the Juilliard School and one of her first professional acting jobs was in 2008 on an episode of “Gossip Girl.”

In 2013, Leigh starred as Nikki Shannon in the series “The Client List” and also appeared in episodes of the TV series “Law & Order” and “Numb3rs.” She was a recurring cast member on the first season of the Bravo reality television series “Vanderpump Rules” in 2013, where she worked as a server at the restaurant SUR.

Leigh is probably best-known for her role in the film “We’re the Millers” which grossed nearly $300 million worldwide in 2013.
