Gajala, her real name was Gazala Shaikh Khan. She is an Indian actress who became famous by her name Gajala. She has predominantly worked in the South Indian film industry where she gave brilliant performances in Tamil and Telugu films. She is notable for her debut Telugu film named ‘Nalo Vunna Prema’ in 2001.

Career

Prior to joining the acting line as a career, Gajala lived in Kuwait. Later, she moved to Mumbai, India. Once in an interview with Times of India, the actress revealed that she never thought to become an actress. Moreover, she revealed that she even decided to become an actress at the age of 40 and 50 years.

In 2001, the actress has worked in ‘Nalo Vunna Prema’ a Telugu debut film. In the film, she appeared as Raaji. In the same year, she gave stand out performances in Student No.1′, ‘O Chinadana’, ‘Kalusukovalani’,      ‘Adrustam’, and ‘Allari Ramudu’.

In 2002, Gajala worked in ‘Ezhumalai’, ‘Bharata Simha’, ‘University’, and ‘Thotti Gang’. Since then, she started getting many films for which she got huge acclaim.

From 2001 to 2011, she was an active actress and is known for her work and performances in many films. She got a positive response from the viewers.
